The Space Place

Welcome to the Space Place

I created this app to learn more about web development and to explore the possibilities of web apps. I coupled this goal with my love for space and astronomy to bring to life a fun and educational app.

As an avid space enthusiast, I'm always looking for new ways to engage with the community and share my passion for the cosmos. After finding dozens of astronomy apps that were either too complicated or not free, I decided to create my own, merging my favorite features into one place.

I hope you enjoy using "The Space Place" as much as I enjoyed creating it!
